The first Islamic app that allows people to read the Quran together and create and manage Khatma in a very simple way. We want people to help and encourage each other to read Quran more. The app will allow you to easily create and share Khatma with your loved ones, it also includes different ways to display the Quran such as Page view or Ayah view to provide you with the best way you can read Quran on the phone without the need for internet. You can also create groups to compete in finishing and memorize Quran, read and listen to supplications (including Hizb el Shazly), listen to your favorite reciters, participate in live audio sessions (we call it circles).
